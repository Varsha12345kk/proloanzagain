Frequently Asked Questions.

How Long Does Approval Take?

Most loans get approved within just 24–48 hours, giving people quick access to the money they need without any long wait. This speedy process helps avoid delays and is especially useful for those who need funds urgently, whether it’s for personal or business reasons. Once you submit your application along with the required documents, the bank quickly reviews them and finishes the checks within this time. This means the loan amount is released faster. The quick approval not only saves time but also gives borrowers peace of mind, knowing their financial needs will be taken care of quickly and smoothly.

What’s The Minimum Loan Amount?

The minimum loan amount depends on the bank’s policy, typically starting from a few thousand units.

Do You Charge Processing Fees?

Yes, a nominal processing fee may be charged depending on the loan type and amount.

Can I Repay Early?

Yes, most banks allow early repayment. Some may charge a small prepayment fee.
